# Lingofy Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the strategic direction for Lingofy over the coming years. Our goal is to evolve from an innovative web app into a comprehensive global language learning ecosystem.

## 🎯 Q3 2026: Mobile Expansion & Stabilization
- **React Native App:** Launch iOS and Android native applications for learning on the go.
- **Offline Capabilities:** Allow users to download cached lyrics, dictionary definitions, and audio tracks to save bandwidth.
- **Advanced Gamification:** Introduce global leaderboards, weekly leagues, and multiplayer pronunciation challenges.

## 🎯 Q4 2026: Enterprise & Education Tier
- **Teacher Dashboard:** A specialized B2B portal where language tutors can monitor their students' pronunciation scores, weak vocabulary, and assign specific songs as homework.
- **Exam Mode:** Simulation environments for IELTS/TOEFL speaking tests using the OpenRouter AI layer.
- **Team Billing:** Multi-seat licenses for schools and universities.

## 🚀 2027: Next-Gen AI & Modalities
- **Real-Time Voice Conversation:** Transition from asynchronous audio recording to real-time WebRTC conversational AI (using Gemini Flash/Whisper Live) simulating native speakers discussing the song's meaning.
- **Video Integration:** Support for music videos (YouTube integration) with spatial lyric syncing.
- **Custom AI Voices:** Ability to clone user voices or select specialized AI voice actors for the Pronunciation Coach.

## 🌍 2028: Global B2B Expansion
- **Publishing Partnerships:** Partner with record labels to provide officially curated curriculum paths based on trending Billboard artists.
- **Corporate Training:** White-label solutions for multinational corporations focusing on business English through podcasts and audiobooks.
- **Analytics Engine:** Deep learning models analyzing cohort drop-off rates, phonetic struggles by native language origin, and predictive learning curves.

---
*Roadmap dates are estimates and subject to change based on community feedback and technological advancements.*
