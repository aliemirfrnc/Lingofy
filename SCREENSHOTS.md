# Screenshot Guide for Investor Deck

To make the pitch deck visually stunning, capture screenshots of the following views. Ensure the app is in "Dark Mode" (if applicable) for a premium aesthetic.

1. **Landing (landing.png)**
   - Hero section with the slogan "Master a new language through the music you already love."
   - The "Connect with Spotify" glowing button.

2. **Login/Auth (login.png)**
   - The minimalist login modal/page showing the email/password fields and the secure lock icon.

3. **Lyrics Sync (lyrics_sync.png)**
   - The main player interface.
   - The current lyric line highlighted and enlarged (showing the O(1) scale effect).
   - Album art and progress bar visible.

4. **Contextual Translation (word_panel.png)**
   - The slide-out or modal panel showing a word's translation.
   - Specifically capture a "Slang" or "Idiom" explanation to highlight the AI's capability.

5. **AI Pronunciation Coach (pronunciation_coach.png)**
   - The microphone recording state.
   - The post-recording result screen showing the accuracy percentage (e.g., "85% - Great Job!") and phoneme breakdown.

6. **Shadowing Mode (shadowing.png)**
   - The full-screen shadowing interface with the lyrics scrolling.

7. **Progress Dashboard (progress.png)**
   - Charts or progress bars showing the user's vocabulary mastery and CEFR level.
   - The list of "Mastered Songs".

8. **Premium Paywall (premium.png)**
   - The pricing tiers (Free, Pro, Master) highlighting the limits and features.

9. **AI Chat Mentor (chat_mentor.png)**
   - A screenshot of the user asking the AI, "What does the artist mean in the chorus?" and the AI responding.

**Tips for taking screenshots:**
- Use a high-resolution display (Retina/4K).
- Use a mock Spotify account playing a globally recognized song (e.g., The Weeknd, Dua Lipa) to make the context relatable to investors.
